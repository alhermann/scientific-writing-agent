## CI-? — Cite original works for specific results

*Severity: major · Check: llm · Scope: global*

Cite the original work that established a specific technical result rather than a review paper. Use review papers only for overviews.

**Rationale:** Citing the original work provides the reader with the most direct and accurate source of the result, while review papers are better suited for summarizing broader contexts.

**How to check:** For each citation that refers to a specific technical result, verify that it points to the original work and not a review.

**Examples:**
- ✗ A review paper is cited for a specific technical result.
- ✓ The original paper that established the result is cited.
