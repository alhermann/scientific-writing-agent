## LG-? — Use 'respectively' sparingly

*Severity: major · Check: llm · Scope: sentence*

Use the word 'respectively' at most once per page and avoid it when the pairing is already clear from context.

**Rationale:** Overuse of 'respectively' forces the reader to mentally parse parallel lists, which can be cognitively taxing and reduce readability.

**How to check:** Scan the document for the word 'respectively' and ensure it is used only when necessary and not in consecutive sentences.

**Examples:**
- ✗ The results for case A and case B are 10% and 20%, respectively. The results for case C and case D are 30% and 40%, respectively.
- ✓ The results for case A and case B are 10% and 20%. For case C and case D, the results are 30% and 40%.
