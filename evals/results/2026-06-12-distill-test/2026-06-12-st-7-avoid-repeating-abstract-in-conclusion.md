## ST-? — Avoid repeating the abstract in the conclusion

*Severity: major · Check: llm · Scope: section*

The conclusion should not restate the abstract verbatim. Instead, it should summarize what was shown and discuss the implications.

**Rationale:** Repeating the abstract in the conclusion is redundant and fails to provide the reader with new insights or a synthesis of the findings.

**How to check:** Compare the conclusion with the abstract and ensure that the conclusion does not simply restate the abstract but instead discusses the results and their significance.

**Examples:**
- ✗ The conclusion is a verbatim copy of the abstract.
- ✓ The conclusion summarizes the results and discusses their implications.
