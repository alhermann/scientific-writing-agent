## MA-? — Check dimensional consistency of equations

*Severity: critical · Check: deterministic · Scope: global*

Before finalizing the manuscript, verify that all equations are dimensionally consistent. Ensure that the units on both sides of the equation match.

**Rationale:** Dimensional inconsistency in equations indicates a fundamental error in the derivation or formulation, which can undermine the credibility of the results.

**How to check:** For each equation, calculate the units of the left-hand side and the right-hand side and confirm they are the same.

**Examples:**
- ✗ The left side of the equation has units of [MPa/s], while the right side has units of [MPa].
- ✓ The left side of the equation has units of [MPa/s], and the right side also has units of [MPa/s].
