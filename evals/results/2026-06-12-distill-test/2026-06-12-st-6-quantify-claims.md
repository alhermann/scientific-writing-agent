## ST-? — Quantify claims or delete them

*Severity: major · Check: llm · Scope: sentence*

When making a claim about results or trends, provide a quantitative measure or remove the sentence entirely.

**Rationale:** Vague statements about results (e.g., 'the model captures the trend') lack specificity and do not convey the strength or significance of the findings.

**How to check:** Review each sentence that makes a claim about results and ensure it includes a numerical value or is removed if it cannot be quantified.

**Examples:**
- ✗ The model captures the experimental trend.
- ✓ The model captures the experimental trend with an R-squared value of 0.95.
